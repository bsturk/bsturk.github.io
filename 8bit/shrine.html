Pictures of my 8 bit setup and computer room

These are just some pictures of the room I spend a *lot* of time in. And with good reason! :) As a kid I always fantasized about a room just for my computer related stuff. Well, here it is!

My workstation(s). Computer on left runs Gentoo Linux. The one on right runs WinXP I have a KVM to switch between the two and two more if needed and an amp that lets me switch inputs so I can get sound from either. I can either stand or sit when using this setup with adjustable monitor and work area.

My 8 bit setup. There's a lot of different configurations I can do here. I can hook up any of the old computers to either the 1702 monitor, TV, or the Amdek monitor. I can also run emulators via TV-out to the same video outputs. I have also built cables to interface some of the old computers to the PC allowing it to act as a file server.

Here are some pictures of the process of getting images between the PC and the old computers. I can do this with just about all of my vintage computers but this particular example shows the C64 and the X1541 cable.

The picture below shows using the excellent Star Commander program to transfer disk images over to the C64.
