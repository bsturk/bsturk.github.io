We are The Brothers Avalon, a 1980's New Wave cover band based out 
of the Seacoast NH area.  We also are known to play some of the 
good "alternative" stuff from the late 80s early 90s.  Think 120 minutes.  
We can go from The Clash to the Pet Shop Boys in the blink of an eye and 
also throw in some of the more well known tunes from the same era 
for good measure.  And because it's all good stuff.  

<br>
Bass and guitar, synthesizers, lots of other instruments, a couple of voices, 
and music that makes anyone smile and remember a good moment in life...
